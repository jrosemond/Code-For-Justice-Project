Our Mission

Founded in 1997, First Hope works to to bring awareness that slavery still exists.

We do this in three ways:

Educate

We EDUCATE on the statistics of Human trafficking.

Equip

We EQUIP with resources to help others get involved.

Encourage

We ENCOURAGE others to participate in the movement.
