Our Purpose in Informing others

We realize that the best way to help each-other is to communicate first and foremost. By talking and informing others in the need to overcome these barriers, we have taken the first step in greatness, which is to work together for the greater good of all people.

With this thought process, we asked ourselves what would have been the most optimal way to provide for others who have little knowledge of human trafficing. This leads into our next step to improve the safety and lives of people who want to make a stand.

Other Organizations

To give people more opportunities to recieve and/or give help, we have partnered with various organizations. These organizations also promote the deconstruction and removal of human trafficing, along with providing aid to people regarding the related materials in the subject matter of human trafficing.

Youth in need of safety, Safe Place is one option.

Safe Place is a national youth outreach and prevention program for young people under the age of 18 (up to 21 years of age in some communities) in need of immediate help and safety. As a collaborative community prevention initiative, Safe Place designates businesses and organizations as Safe Place locations, making help readily available to youth in communities across the country.

Children and female survivors can recieve care at FAIR Girls.

FAIR Girls initiates intervention and holistic care to female survivors of human trafficking and commercial sexual exploitation of children. Since our founding in 2003, FAIR Girls has served well over 1,200 girls and young women, providing safe housing, client-centered, trauma-informed direct services, and the life skills they need to transition from victim to survivor.

If you want to end the sexual exploitation of children, ECPAT-USA is one of the largest organizations in stopping it.

The ECPAT-USA is the leading anti-trafficking policy organization in the United States. Nearly 30 years ago, ECPAT-USA became the first U.S.-based nonprofit to work on the issue of commercial sexual exploitation of children.

Men who have suffered from sexual abuse or assault, MaleSurvivor is one of the largest organizations in stopping it.

For 26 years, MaleSurvivor has fostered a healing community where tens of thousands of men from more than 200 countries come together to find support, information, and treatment. MaleSurvivor is a non-profit, public benefit organization committed to preventing, healing, and eliminating all forms of sexual victimization of boys and men through support, treatment, research, education, advocacy, and activism.
